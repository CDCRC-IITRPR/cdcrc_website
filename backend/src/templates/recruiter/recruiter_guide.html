Recruiter Guide
1

Welcome to CDPC IIT Ropar! We look forward to working with your organization. Contact us on placement@iitrpr.ac.in for any placement or internship related queries or give a call to any of our team members at any of the numbers mentioned on the team page.

2

We'll request you to give the details of the profiles that you'll be recruiting for by filling the Job Announcement Form (for placements) and the Internship Notification Form (for internships) to formally kickstart the recruiting process.

3

Once you have filled the JAF or INF, we will get in touch with you to work out a suitable slot which works for your organization. Meanwhile, we will quickly collect the data of the students eligible for interviewing for the profiles that you have floated.

4

The recruitment process will be carried out during the agreed upon slot. Given the COVID-19 situation this year, we are fully geared to conduct all the processes online as per convenience.

5

Once the students have been selected, we'll help you out with all the onboarding formalities where required.
