It is my pleasure to introduce and invite you to the Indian Institute of Technology Ropar for various placement and internship options. IIT Ropar has evolved as one of the top-ranking institutes in India and Globally in a short span of time. The global rank of IIT Ropar has improved over the years, and one of the reasons for it is the excellent academic program offered in our campus. The backbone of our institute is the students and the young faculty having new and innovative ideas. Our faculty collaborates with highly reputed organizations that allow our students to learn from live R&D projects.

Students in our campus are engaged in well devised classroom and laboratory activities and active participation in R&D and industrial projects. IIT Ropar’s curriculum has been designed to provide strong technical knowledge, and at the same time, it also nurtures a sense of responsibility and leadership among students. Further, fostering leadership quality, students are encouraged to participate in student societies, competitions, social service activities, and entrepreneurship from the early stage.

At IIT Ropar, several options for industrial and research internships are available, which have been crafted to accord with the requirements of the different industries at various levels. Our students are ready to take any challenging task given to them, and I am confident that wherever our students will be provided with opportunities, they will outshine. With this, I once again invite you to our campus for placement and internship options, your recruitment team will have a very productive and satisfying visit to our institute.

(Education is that which provides Freedom)
Jai Hind!
